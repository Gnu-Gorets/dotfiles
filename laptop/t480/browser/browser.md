# LibreWolf - Browser

1. Copy [user.js](user.js) in root of profile in Librewolf
2. Copy [chrome](chrome) in root of profile in LibreWolf
3. Restore addons data from backup
4. [For RU only] https://antizapret.prostovpn.org/proxy.pac
5. Change $USER to your username in [user.js](user.js)
